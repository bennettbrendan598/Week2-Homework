Oswego, New York

This is my hometown! Although it will always have a special place in my heart, I will never return (LOL!) Just across the lake from Ontario, Canada (which you could actually see on a clear day), this extremely small town is where I grew up. Thankfully my dad took me away from the town before I was a teenager; if he didn't, my life probably would've been the complete opposite of what it is now.
Fairfax, Virginia

Just outside of Washington D.C., this beautiful town had plenty to see and do! I loved going to the Smithsonian Mall, and seeing all the sights of our country's Capitol, but still not a place I would settle down in.
Destin, Florida

This is where I went through the majority of my teenage years. It was a beach, tourist town in the panhandle of Florida! I made a lot of great friends there, but it was kind of a hard place to be given that our socioeconomic status was much, much, much lower than my peers. I go back to visit occasionally, as I do still have some relatives in the area.
Orlando, Florida

Although this isn't where I technically "grew up," it is where I, without a doubt, did the most growing. I attended the University of Central Florida, and constantly partied. It was fun for my "coming of age" years, but I was eager to part ways with my lifestyle I was living.
Denver, Colorado

Finally! Somewhere that feels like home! My boyfriend and I moved here because we've both always wanted to live out west! There's an outstanding music scene, beautiful nature all around, and some of the country's best skiing! I could not be happier than I am living here.
