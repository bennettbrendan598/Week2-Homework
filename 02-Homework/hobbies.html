Music Production and Disk Jockeying

Music is a huge, huge, huge part of my life! I spend most of my summers traveling the country (and even outside the country) going to different festivals and gatherings. Since I developed this passion at the young age of 15, I have been working on creating my own sound. I love producing and DJ'ing house music. House music makes me feel a part of a larger community, and truly feeds my soul. In addition to this genre, I love others such as: glitch-hop, trip-hop, techno, acid-scratch, deep bass, jam, and so much more!
Hiking, Camping, and other Outdoor Activities

I love to stay active! I'm not really a "go-to-the-gym-5-times-a-week" type, but I LOVE taking beautiful hikes, or camping in scenic locations. Living in Denver is great because we are centralized between so many breathtaking destinations in the United States!
